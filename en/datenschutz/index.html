Website Provision

For the technical provision and ensuring the functionality of our website.

Contact

To process your inquiries and communicate with you.

Legal Compliance

To fulfill legal retention obligations and enforce rights.

Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your personal data is based on the following legal bases:

Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (Legitimate Interest)

For the technical provision of the website and to ensure security.

Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR (Contract Performance)

To fulfill pre-contractual measures and contract performance when contacting us.

Storage Duration

Your personal data will only be stored for as long as necessary for the respective purposes or as required by legal retention periods. Technical log data is usually deleted after 30 days. Data from contact inquiries will not be automatically deleted.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website does not use cookies and no tracking technologies. No data is stored on your end device and no analysis of your user behavior takes place.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to obtain information about your stored personal data.

Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

You have the right to request the correction of incorrect or completion of incomplete data.

Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, provided no legal retention obligations exist.

Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

You have the right to request the restriction of processing of your data.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interest.

Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7 para. 3 GDPR)

You have the right to withdraw any consent given at any time.

Right to Lodge a Complaint (Art. 77 GDPR)

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the member state of your residence, workplace, or the place of the alleged violation.

Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from loss, destruction, manipulation, and unauthorized access. Our security measures are continuously improved in line with technological progress.

Disclosure to Third Parties

Your personal data is generally not disclosed to third parties. Exceptions only exist if:

Hosting Provider

For the technical provision of the website (server hosting within the EU/EEA).

Email Provider

For the transmission of emails (within the EU/EEA, GDPR-compliant).

Data Transfer to Third Countries

A transfer of your data to third countries outside the EU/EEA generally does not occur. Should this be necessary in exceptional cases, this will only take place on the basis of adequate guarantees (e.g., standard contractual clauses of the EU Commission) or your explicit consent.
