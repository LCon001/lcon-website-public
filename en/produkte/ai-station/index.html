Use Cases

AI Station sets you free.

Corporate Memory

The end of endless searches for documents and information.

The challenge

You have hundreds or thousands of documents (quality manuals, ISO standards, HR policies, customer contracts, etc.), but nobody finds the right information quickly.

The solution

We connect your documents to the local AI Station. Your staff simply ask via chat: "How do I enter my travel times for a business trip?" or "What are the fire safety requirements per ÖNORM for our warehouse?" and get the exact answer with a link to the source document. Perfect for quick research and employee onboarding.

The Data Privacy Dilemma

AI without US corporations.

The challenge

You want to simplify workflows with AI, but privacy, GDPR, or the EU AI Act worry you? You want to use AI's potential without starting from scratch and having to learn everything first?

The solution

Our AI Station—a closed, 100% local AI platform—combined with our expert service provides the answer. We analyze your tasks and build targeted AI apps that handle your specific routine work.

Process & Deadline Monitoring

Never chase missing documents again.

The challenge

Real processes stall because external inputs are missing (e.g. receipts from clients, plans from subcontractors, tenant self-disclosures). Follow-ups consume enormous time.

The solution

An AI app automatically checks the status of customer or employee cases via various interfaces (e.g. email, CRM, ERP, …) against defined checklists. If documents are missing, the system generates and sends polite, personalized reminders. Your back office is massively relieved.

24/7 Support

Instant answers to standard questions.

The challenge

Your phone rings constantly or your inbox overflows with the same organizational questions from customers, partners, or patients.

The solution

A local AI app answers technical and organizational routine questions around the clock. "What format must I use for the application?", "Where is the upload portal?", or "What documents should I bring to the first meeting?" Your strategic and expert advice remains with your team.
